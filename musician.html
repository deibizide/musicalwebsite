Teaching Ethos

David's teaching ethos is that each pupil is an individual and will be taught accordingly. He is a strong believer in teaching to promote confidence and abilities. Passing this philosophy onto his pupils is a fundamental aspect of his teaching. Once you fall in love with an instrument, the other attributes such as technique and theory all fall into place naturally! Not only can learning a musical instrument or vocal tuition help develop various mental skills, it can also help with other areas of learning and development, as well as increase confidence.
